About Me

Wealth isn't one-size-fits-all — and neither is the path to owning it.

You deserve more than a lender who processes your file. You deserve a strategist who knows your name, understands your story, and connects you to the right resources at the right time. I'm Aisha Harris — mortgage broker, real estate investor, and founder of AROE.

I've shown up for families, entrepreneurs, veterans, and survivors — because everyone deserves a seat at the table.

Whether you're stepping into your first home, scaling your investment portfolio, launching your next business chapter, or rebuilding after an unexpected setback — I bring the strategy, the network, and the resources to move you forward.

This isn't just what I do. It's my purpose.

Let's build your legacy together.

"The right connection, at the right time, changes everything — and everyone deserves that moment."
